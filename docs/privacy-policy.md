# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 13, 2026**

UltraWin is a macOS app that lets you share only part of your screen in video
calls. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. In short: your
screen content never leaves your Mac, and the only thing that ever does is
anonymous, non-personal usage statistics.

## The short version

- UltraWin captures your screen **only** to mirror your selected region onto a
  local virtual display. **No frame is ever uploaded, recorded, or stored** —
  by us or anyone else.
- We collect a small amount of **anonymous usage analytics** that cannot
  identify you.
- No accounts, no ads, no tracking across apps or websites, and we never sell
  your data.

## Screen Recording permission

macOS requires the Screen Recording permission for any app that reads screen
content. UltraWin needs it for exactly one thing: continuously copying the
pixels of your selected region onto the invisible "UltraWin Display" that you
share in your meeting app. That happens entirely on your Mac, in memory:

- no frame ever leaves your device,
- nothing is recorded or written to disk,
- the developer has no access to anything you share.

## Data stored on your device

UltraWin stores only its settings — your selected hotkey, dim level, aspect
lock, and whether you've seen the onboarding — locally in macOS user defaults.
If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

## Anonymous analytics

To understand how UltraWin is used and to improve it, the app collects a small
amount of **anonymous** usage data through
[TelemetryDeck](https://telemetrydeck.com), a privacy-focused analytics service.

What's collected:

- when a sharing session starts or stops, whether it was started from the
  hotkey or the menu bar, and whether the 16:9 snap was on;
- when the first-run onboarding is completed.

What's **not** collected:

- your screen content — no pixels, window titles, app names, or region
  coordinates;
- your name, email, IP address, or any personal identifier;
- your precise location;
- any advertising or cross-app tracking identifier.

TelemetryDeck derives an anonymous, salted identifier for each installation so
that usage can be counted without identifying anyone. This identifier cannot be
traced back to you, and the data is not linked to your identity. TelemetryDeck
is GDPR- and CCPA-compliant; you can read their privacy policy at
<https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/>.

## Network usage

The app connects to the network only to:

- send the anonymous analytics signals described above,
- check for app updates against a static file hosted on GitHub Pages
  ([Sparkle](https://sparkle-project.org) — no personal data is sent),
- load the developer avatar shown in the About tab (a public GitHub image).

No other data is transmitted.

## No accounts, ads, or selling of data

UltraWin has no user accounts and no login. It contains no advertising, no
third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we never sell or share your data
with data brokers.

## Children's privacy

UltraWin is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
personal information from anyone.

## Your choices and rights

Because the analytics data is fully anonymous, it cannot be associated with you
and therefore cannot be individually identified, exported, or deleted on
request. You can stop all analytics collection by removing the app. Your local
settings are under your control and are deleted when you remove the app.
