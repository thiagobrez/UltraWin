UltraWin

Your whole screen is too much to share.

Share only part of your ultrawide screen in video calls. Your apps stay exactly where they are.

Download for macOS
$ brew install thiagobrez/tap/ultrawin

Requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. Free and open source.

UltraWin Display

An invisible second display

Drag-select any region of your screen. UltraWin creates an invisible virtual display that continuously mirrors it — no window to babysit, nothing to rearrange.

Everything outside your region dims, so you always know what's shared.

UltraWin Display

Works with every meeting app

In Zoom, Meet, or Teams, share the screen named “UltraWin Display”.
Everyone sees just your region — never the rest of your screen.

Move and resize while live

Sharing something that moved? Drag the frame's edges to reposition it and corners to resize it — the shared output follows in real time.

Hold ⌘ and drag to move the whole selection.

⌘ ⇧ U

One hotkey, start to stop

Press it from any app to select a region — press it again to stop sharing.

You can change it anytime in Preferences.
